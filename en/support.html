Support

Online Help Desk

The Help Desk is available to clients who have purchased WorkflowGen.

Before posting a ticket, be sure to:

1. Check if the answer is available in the Knowledge Base (Forum).
2. Try to reproduce the error.
3. Get the error information (screen shots, a copy of the error message, etc.).
4. Have your configuration information ready.
