Training

Overview

The agenda of the workflow software training can be customized based on your requirements and the profile of the participants. During the sessions the trainer usually works with the participants on the implementation of their first processes.

The training sessions can be performed online or on-site at the location of your choice.
Here is a presentation of the different profiles involved in a WorkflowGen project and the topics covered for each during the training. Because of the simplicity of the product, no training is required for end-users.
